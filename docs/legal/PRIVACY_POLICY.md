## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
Information that identifies you as an individual:
- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
- **Account Credentials**: Username, password (encrypted)
- **Identity Verification**: Date of birth, Social Security Number (if required for underwriting)
- **Payment Information**: Credit card details (processed and stored by Stripe, not by us)

### 2.2 Pet Information
Information about your pet(s):
- **Identification**: Name, species (dog/cat), breed, age, gender
- **Physical Characteristics**: Weight, color, microchip number
- **Health Information**: 
  - Medical history (vaccinations, surgeries, treatments)
  - Pre-existing conditions
  - Current medications
  - Allergies

- **Behavioral Information**: Neutered status, indoor/outdoor lifestyle

### 2.3 Policy and Claims Data
- **Quote Information**: Coverage selections, premium amounts, risk scores
- **Policy Details**: Policy number, effective dates, coverage terms

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system

- **Location Data**: Geographic location (city, state, zip code)
- **Cookies and Tracking**: See Section 3 below

### 2.5 AI Interaction Data
- **Conversational Data**: Your responses to Pawla (our AI assistant) during the quote flow
- **AI Decisions**: Risk scores, eligibility determinations, AI reasoning
- **Feedback**: Your ratings of AI interactions (sentiment feedback)

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption (HTTPS)
- **Database Encryption**: Sensitive data encrypted at rest in Firebase
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access (admin, customer, support)
- **Authentication**: Secure login with password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Firestore Security Rules**: 310+ lines of database access control rules

### 7.2 Payment Security
- **PCI Compliance**: Payment processing handled by Stripe (PCI Level 1 certified)
- **No Card Storage**: We do not store full credit card numbers
- **Tokenization**: Payment methods are tokenized by Stripe

### 7.3 Data Breach Response
In the event of a data breach:
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours (as required by GDPR)
- We will notify relevant regulatory authorities
- We will provide guidance on protective measures you should take

### 7.4 No Guarantee
**No security system is 100% secure.** While we implement industry-standard security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Retention Periods
- **Active Policies**: Data retained for the duration of the policy plus 7 years (regulatory requirement)
- **Expired Policies**: Data retained for 7 years after expiration
- **Claims**: Data retained for 10 years after claim closure
- **Quotes (No Policy)**: Data retained for 3 years
- **Account Data**: Data retained while account is active, then 1 year after closure

### 8.2 Deletion Requests
- You may request deletion of your personal data
- We will honor requests unless we have a legal obligation to retain data
