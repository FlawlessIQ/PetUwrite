## 5. Pre-Existing Condition Disclaimer

**PRE-EXISTING CONDITIONS ARE NOT COVERED.**

### 5.1 Definition
A pre-existing condition is any illness, injury, or medical condition that:
- Existed before the policy effective date
- Showed signs or symptoms before coverage began
- Was diagnosed or treated before the policy start date
- Would have been apparent to a reasonable person

### 5.2 Examples of Pre-Existing Conditions
- Chronic conditions (arthritis, diabetes, allergies)
- Hereditary conditions (hip dysplasia, heart murmurs)
- Congenital conditions (birth defects)
- Any condition for which veterinary care was sought or recommended before coverage

### 5.3 Waiting Periods
- **Accident Coverage**: Typically immediate (no waiting period)
- **Illness Coverage**: 14-30 day waiting period
- **Orthopedic Conditions**: 6-12 month waiting period
- **Specific Conditions**: Waiting periods vary by condition and carrier

**Conditions that arise during waiting periods are considered pre-existing and are not covered.**

## 6. Coverage Limitations and Exclusions

### 6.1 Standard Exclusions
The following are **typically NOT covered** (refer to your policy for complete exclusions):
- Pre-existing conditions
- Routine and preventive care (wellness visits, vaccinations) unless you purchase a wellness add-on
- Breeding, pregnancy, and whelping/queening
- Cosmetic procedures (tail docking, ear cropping, declawing)
- Behavioral issues (training, anxiety) unless you purchase behavioral coverage
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Intentional injury or neglect
- War, nuclear hazards, or acts of terrorism

### 6.2 Breed-Specific Exclusions
Certain breeds may have **additional exclusions**:
- **Brachycephalic Breeds** (Bulldogs, Pugs): Respiratory conditions may be excluded
- **Large Breeds** (Great Danes, Mastiffs): Bloat or gastric torsion may have limited coverage
- **High-Risk Breeds**: Some breeds may be ineligible for coverage

### 6.3 Age Restrictions
- **Minimum Age**: Pets must typically be at least 8 weeks old
- **Maximum Age**: Pets over 14 years old may be ineligible for new coverage
- **Senior Pet Limitations**: Pets over 10 years may have reduced coverage options

## 7. Claim Payment Disclaimer

### 7.1 Reimbursement Model
- Pet insurance operates on a **reimbursement basis**
- You pay the veterinary bill upfront
- You submit a claim for reimbursement
- Reimbursement is based on your policy terms (deductible, co-pay, coverage limits)

### 7.2 Claim Approval Not Guaranteed
- Claims are subject to review and approval by the insurance carrier
- Claims may be denied if:
  - Condition is pre-existing
  - Treatment is excluded under your policy
  - Documentation is insufficient
  - Policy was not active at time of treatment
  - Annual or per-incident limits exceeded

### 7.3 Claim Processing Time
- **Standard Claims**: 5-10 business days
- **Complex Claims**: Up to 30 days
- **Rush Processing**: Not available for emergency claims; process time is the same
